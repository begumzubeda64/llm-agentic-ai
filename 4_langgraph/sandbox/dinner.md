# Dinner Report: Il Ristorante-Niko Romito

## Restaurant Details
- **Name:** Il Ristorante-Niko Romito  
- **Address:** Jumeira Bay Island, Jumeira 2, Dubai, UAE  
- **Phone:** +971 4 777 5433  

## Menu Highlights
Il Ristorante-Niko Romito offers a refined Italian dining experience with a focus on classic Italian dishes presented in a contemporary style. Some standout dishes include:  
- Lobster pasta  
- Risotto  
- Various vegetarian options such as spaghetti with tomato  
- The 'Degustazione' tasting menu available for a comprehensive culinary experience  

## Reviews
Several reviews describe the restaurant as having an upscale ambiance reminiscent of its Milan counterpart. Guests have praised the impeccable service and presentation of the food; however, some reviews mention that while the dishes are visually appealing, the taste did not always meet expectations. Common sentiments reflected in reviews include:
- **Positive:** Excellent service, beautiful ambiance, standout dishes (like lobster pasta)
- **Mixed:** Some dishes did not meet taste expectations, described as average compared to other Michelin-starred dining experiences.

Overall, it is a well-reviewed dining option for those seeking an Italian culinary experience in Dubai, though it may lean towards the pricier side.
